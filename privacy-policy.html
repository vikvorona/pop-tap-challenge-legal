Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 29, 2025

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

We do not require you to create an account or provide personal information to play Pop Tap Challenge.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

The Game may collect:

  • Game progress and scores (stored locally on your device)
  • Device performance information for optimization
  • Crash reports and error logs to improve stability

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Save your game progress locally on your device
  • Provide leaderboard functionality through Game Center
  • Improve game performance and fix bugs
  • Display relevant advertisements

3. Game Center Integration

If you choose to use Game Center integration:

  • Your scores may be submitted to Game Center leaderboards
  • Your Game Center profile information may be visible to other players
  • This data is governed by Apple's Game Center Privacy Policy

4. Local Data Storage

Your game progress, settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device using Unity's PlayerPrefs system. This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 Advertisement Partners

We use ironSource LevelPlay to display advertisements. These services may collect:

  • Device advertising identifiers
  • Usage patterns for ad targeting
  • Device information for ad optimization

Please review ironSource's Privacy Policy at https://developers.is.com/ironsource-mobile/air/ironsource-mobile-privacy-policy/

5.2 Apple Game Center

Game Center integration is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy: https://apple.com/privacy/

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. Children's Privacy

Our Game does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will delete it promptly.

8. Your Rights

You can:

  • Delete the Game to remove all locally stored data
  • Disable Game Center integration in your device settings
  • Adjust ad preferences through your device's privacy settings

9. Data Retention

Game progress data is stored locally on your device until you delete the Game or reset your device. We do not store this data on our servers.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the Game.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the App Store or Google Play Store review and support systems where you downloaded the game.

12. International Users

This Game is intended for users worldwide. By using the Game, you consent to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.
