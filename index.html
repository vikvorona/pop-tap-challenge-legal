How to Play

  1. Watch for green circles - They appear randomly on the grid
  2. Tap quickly - You have 3 seconds before they disappear
  3. Avoid distractions - Don't tap other colored circles in later levels
  4. Progress through levels - Each level gets more challenging
  5. Beat your high score - How far can you go?

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: The game isn't loading or crashes on startup
A: Try restarting the app completely. If issues persist, restart your device and ensure you have the latest iOS version.
Q: My taps aren't registering
A: Make sure to tap directly on the green circles. Clean your screen and ensure your device's touch sensitivity is working properly.
Q: How many levels are there?
A: There are 50 levels total, each progressively more challenging than the last.
Q: Can I pause the game?
A: Each level is designed to be completed in one session. You can exit and return to the level selection screen at any time.
Q: What devices are supported?
A: PopIt Challenge is designed for iOS devices running iOS 12.0 or later.
Q: Is this game suitable for children?
A: Yes! The game is family-friendly and great for improving reflexes and concentration at any age.

Tips for Success

  • Stay focused - Distracting colored circles appear in later levels
  • Practice regularly - Your reflexes will improve over time
  • Start with easier levels - Build confidence before tackling harder challenges
  • Take breaks - Rest your eyes and maintain concentration
